Why I keep my sites boring

I like static sites because they are predictable: cheap to host, fast, and easy to secure.

Less moving parts

A static site removes a whole class of failure: databases, servers, patching, and dependency drift. For a personal site, that is exactly what I want.

Better caching

CloudFront loves static content. It is fast globally and trivial to invalidate when I publish.

Security by simplicity

Fewer dynamic endpoints means a smaller attack surface. No auth. No sessions. No surprises.
